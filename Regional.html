Regional Activities

Cubing India is a non-profit organization that mainly works through the grassroot efforts of our volunteers. This organization is dedicated to support and develop speedcubing community in India. We help teams of volunteers and regional cubing communities organize events at different parts of the country to make it accessible to as many cubers as possible. Let’s take time to thank and appreciate the work of our members, without whose help cubing culture couldn’t have been a wide spread success in India.
